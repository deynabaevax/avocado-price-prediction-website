Goal

As an avocado-lover myself, I have always been interested in the shifting changes in avocados prices. I have noticed that their prices are not fixed, and there are periods when the prices are rising steadily. That is why I have chosen to make a model for predicting avocado prices.

The dataset

The dataset that I have chosen for the challenge is "Avocado Prices (2020)" from Kaggle.
